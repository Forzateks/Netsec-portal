OT Policy Reference
Eve (8PM–11:59PM weekday, no midnight cross): 1:1 always. 8 credited hrs = 1 Comp Off day.
Eve/Split (starts 8PM+, crosses midnight): Eve portion at 1:1 + Midnight portion doubled at 1:2. Cap 8hrs total.
Early Morning (5AM–9AM weekday): 1:1 always. Only hours up to 9:00 AM counted. No CO earned.
Midnight (crosses midnight before 8PM, or starts before 5AM): <4hrs=1:1, ≥4hrs=1:2. Cap 8hrs.
Weekend — UAE (Sat/Sun) or KSA (Fri/Sat): <4hrs=1:1, ≥4hrs=1:2. Cap 8hrs. Earns CO.
Comp Off rules: Full Day=1 day, Half Day=0.5. Submit request → Manager approves → appears in balance. No monetary payout. No WFH.
Annual Leave: 22 working days per year. Sick Leave: 15 days per year. Submit request → Manager approves → deducted from balance.
